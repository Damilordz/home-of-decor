OUR STORY

Transforming Spaces with innovative designs, Explore our story of craftmanship
and style while we create furnitures that inspires and enhance modern standard of living.

From Humble Beginning

Our story began in 2022, where different individuals who had
passion for creating beautiful and comfortable furniture joined
hands together, with the aim of making this a known brand for
quality and designs.

Innovation and Growth

Innovation has always been the heart of what we
do. in 2024, we hope to introduce our first line of
eco-friendly furniture, crafted from sustainable
materials. we hope that this commitment to the
environment will earn us accolades and also
loyalty and trust from prospective clients and
customers.

Milestones & Achievements

We would be proud to mark significant
milestones in our journey as a leading furniture
brand. We hope to be recognized for
innovative and sustainable designs with
multiple industry accolades, affirming our
commitment to excellence.

Our Global Reach

Home of decor would love to serve clients all over the
world. With distribution centers in key locations while
making sure that our high quality furniture reaches
your respective homes effectively and safely. Our Global
presence would be a testament to the love and support of our
customers who inspired us to start this amazing journey.

Looking Ahead

As we look to the future our goal remains the same : To create
furnitures that combines style, comfort and sustainability. We
are exicited about the new designs and innovations we have in
store, and are committed to making your home a beautiful,
comfortable and happy place.
